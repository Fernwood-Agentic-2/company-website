about

Fernwood agentic is a startup based in Victoria, Canada. We build policy-focused language model systems for complex policy libraries. Our first clients have been organizations working in the humanitarian aid sector. Our first product, Living Index, was made for a humanitarian policy organization seeking to build safer AI for child protection and education in emergencies. It transforms long, fragmented policy and research libraries into practical tools that experts can query in plain language. We are now working on a system for land use policy called Spatial, working with local architects and city planners to design our data structure.
